Safe Deposit Boxes

Take comfort in knowing your valuables are safe with a waterproof and fireproof safe deposit box.  You can safely store your important documents, jewelry, family heirlooms, etc. Multiple box sizes are available at our Pascagoula, Moss Point, Gautier and Ocean Springs branches.

Box SizeAnnual Fee
3"x5" £18.00
3"x10" £30.00
5"x10" £42.00
7"x10" £60.00*
10"x10" £100.00*

*Available only at Pascagoula location.

Telephone Banking

Check your account balances, transfer money and review recent transactions at any time with Siam Bank ’s convenient 24 hour telephone banking. Call 1-888-729-6434.

Report a Lost or Stolen Card

Lost or misplaced your debit card? No worries! Call the Fraud Prevention Center at 800-554-8969 to report it, and then call Siam Bank at +44-000-000-0000 or 844-769-5305 to order your new debit card.
