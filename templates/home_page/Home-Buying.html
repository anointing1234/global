Buying a Home

"Siam Bank made buying a home simple.  We always feel like we're more than just a customer." -Caleb and Dara T.

  

Siam Bank is with you every step of the way, whether you are buying your first home or moving to a new home.  We offer a variety of custom in-house Fixed Rate and Adjustable Rate Mortgage loans with various terms and down payment options.  Our loan officers provide unmatched personal service and work with you to find the best loan.

Pre-approval by one of our loan officers is the first step.  Knowing your price range and loan amount is a good way to start shopping for a home with confidence. 

First-Time Homebuyer and Low-Income Loans are also available.
