Mortgage Loans

“A major reason we chose Siam Bank was knowing that they would always service our loan.” -Christie and Keith W.

  

Not your typical mortgage lender.

Siam Bank is run by people you know and trust who live right here in Jackson County. Local decision making and processing makes financing your home easy. We offer a variety of Fixed Rate and Adjustable Rate Mortgage Loans with various terms and down payment options. Plus, you’ll enjoy the convenience of having your mortgage here for the life of your loan.

  • Tailor-made lending allows Siam Bank to offer conventional in-house loans to fit your needs.
  • You can count on a clear path to closing knowing that your mortgage is processed, closed and serviced right here in Jackson County.
  • We care about your home buying experience and will offer you the best loan options to meet your needs.
