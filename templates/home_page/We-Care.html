Because You’re First With Us...  

When you grow, we grow.

  

For over 65 years, Global Trust Bank has been committed to the communities in which we live. We have fostered that commitment by supporting a variety of local charities and events.  Our customers are our neighbors and our goal is to build loyal relationships with you, our employees and the community.  

The primary goal of our outreach is to change lives in our communities through doing good and giving back to causes meaningful to our employees and customers.
